Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Account Information: Username, email address, and encrypted password
  • Health Data: Dengue experience reports, symptoms, and prevention activities (voluntarily provided)
  • Location Data: City/region information for risk assessment purposes
Automatically Collected Information
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the service
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Session Data: Login times, user preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses
  • Provide dengue risk assessments
  • Generate personalized recommendations
  • Improve prediction accuracy
  • Maintain user accounts and preferences
Secondary Uses
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve service
  • Generate anonymized research insights
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure service security and integrity

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data.
Limited Sharing Occurs Only When:
  • Legal Requirements: Required by law, court order, or government request
  • Health Emergency: Public health authorities require aggregate data for outbreak response
  • Service Providers: Third-party services necessary for platform operation (hosting, analytics)
  • Research Purposes: Anonymized, aggregated data for dengue research (with explicit consent)

4. Data Security

Protection Measures
  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Control: Restricted access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and vulnerability assessments
  • Data Minimization: Collect only necessary information
While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Data Rights
  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
Privacy Choices
  • Marketing: Opt-out of promotional communications
  • Analytics: Disable usage tracking
  • Location: Control location data sharing
  • Cookies: Manage cookie preferences

6. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period Purpose
Account Information Duration of account + 1 year Account management, legal compliance
Health Experience Data 5 years or until deletion requested Research, trend analysis
Usage Analytics 2 years Service improvement
System Logs 90 days Security, troubleshooting

7. Cookies and Tracking

Types of Cookies Used
  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality (login, security)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the service
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

Managing Cookies: You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling certain cookies may limit functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately so we can remove such information.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do:

  • We will notify you via email or prominent notice on our service
  • The "Last updated" date will be revised
  • For material changes, we may require re-consent
  • Continued use after changes indicates acceptance

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, concerns, or requests:

  • Email: privacy@denguerisk.com
  • Support: support@denguerisk.com
  • Response Time: Within 30 days
  • Location: Karnataka, India
